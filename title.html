Department of Computer Science, University of Ghana

Objective

The objective of the Department of Computer Science is to provide an academic environment that fosters intellectual growth and innovation in the field of computer science. Our faculty is dedicated to delivering a high-quality education, preparing our students for successful careers in academia and industry.

Departmental Courses

The Department of Computer Science offers a wide range of courses that cover the fundamental principles of computer science. Some of the key courses include:

  • Introduction to Programming
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Software Engineering

Faculty and Staff

Our dedicated faculty members have extensive experience in their respective fields and are actively involved in research and development. The department also employs a support staff to ensure the smooth functioning of academic programs and administrative tasks.

Student Opportunities

The Department of Computer Science offers a variety of opportunities for students to engage in research and gain practical experience. Some of these opportunities include:

  • Research Assistantships
  • Teaching Assistantships
  • Summer Research Programs
  • Industry Internships
  • Participation in National and International Conferences

Facilities

The Department of Computer Science is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities that provide our students with the necessary resources to excel in their studies. These facilities include:

  • Computer Labs with access to modern programming languages and tools
  • Multimedia Labs for audio and video editing and production
  • Networking and Server Labs for hands-on learning
  • Library with access to a comprehensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources

Achievements

Our department has a rich history of achievements and we are proud of our students who have made outstanding contributions to the field of computer science. Some of these achievements include:

  • Securing top ranks in national and international exams
  • Publishing research papers in prestigious conferences and journals
  • Winning national and international competitions
  • Receiving prestigious scholarships and fellowships
